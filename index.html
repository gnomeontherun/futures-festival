Full Spectrum Futures

Saturday October 24, 2020 07:00 to 19:00 UTC

Register Now!

2020 continues to change our lives in dramatic ways. Globally.

To use our theme from last year as inspiration, 'radical transformations' are unfolding in front of us. There is an aftermath of grief, pain, and loss, but also that of new possibilities.

Our festival theme this year, Full Spectrum Futures, will amplify practitioner's and projects who are driving change in partnership with the community. Who, in the chaos, are seeing windows where there are walls. Who are asking new types of questions. Whose projects are welcoming new voices into the conversation, and the action.
